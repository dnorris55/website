NINTH GENERATION

364. Robert JACKSON Sr. was born on 17 Mar 1733 in Femmanagh, Ireland. He died on 28 Sep 1809 in Searights, Fayette Co, PA. He was buried in Searights Episco Cemetery. BIOGRAPHY: DAR #'s 33261, 89557, 195128, 216985, 219985. DAR Index gives the following information: JACKSON, Robert Sr: b 3-17-1733 IR d 9-28-1809 PA m Mary Hewthorhn Pvt PA. No record of pension application was found in the Index of Rev. War Pension Applications. Will in Fayette Co, Wills, Vol 1, Pg 126 ( I assume this is Fayette Co, PA).

BIOGRAPHY: DAR Apl 195128 gives fathers name as Hugh and his place of birth as Ireland. It also says Robert was awarded land grant of 300 3/4 acres in Fayette Co, PA, Feb 7, 1785.

He was married to Mary Ann HENTHORN on 25 Dec 1756 in Fayette Co, PA.
