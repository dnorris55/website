SEVENTH GENERATION

90. William GRIFFITH was born on 26 Apr 1778 in Bucks Co, Pa. He died on 7 Apr 1854 in Woodsfield, Monroe Co, OH. This William Griffith d. 26 Apr. 1854 Monroe Co., Oh. m. Mary :Polly" Jackson b. 1790 Fayette Co., Pa. d. 1888 Monroe Co., Oh. Their children are:
Abraham Griffith b. 1806 Pa.
Lewis Griffith b. 1811 Pa.
Benjamin Griffith d. 1850
Jackson Griffith b. 1818 Monroe Co., Oh.
Sarah Ann Griffith b. 1820 Monroe Co., Oh.
David Griffith b. 1832 Monroe Co., Oh.
This David m. Sarah Ann Jackson b. 1825 Oh.
He was married to Mary JACKSON in 1807.

91. Mary JACKSON was born on 20 May 1790 in Menallen Twp, Fayette, PA. She died on 29 Feb 1880 in Center Twp, Monroe County, Ohio. She was buried in Neuhart Cemetery, Monroe Co, OH. Children were:

40 i. Abraham "Abram" GRIFFITH.
ii. Lewis GRIFFITH was born in 1811 in PA. He died before 1870.
iii. Benjamin GRIFFITH died in 1850. Hi Lowell, The only info I have is that Benjamin Griffith was married to Sarah Ann Stephens b. 4 Jan. 1817 d. 13 Dec. 1907 Center Twp., Monroe Co., Oh.. She is the dau. of David Stephens b.1770 Pa. d. 18 Jul. 1844 Center Twp., Monroe Co., Oh. and Rebecca Jackson b. 8 Nov. 1782 Pa. d. 4 Mar. 1872 Center Twp., Monroe Co., Oh. This would make them 1st cousins as Benjamin's mother and Sarah's mother were sisters. I believe, they are the daughters of Abraham Jackson s/o Robert Jackson as he has two daughters by these names with the same birth dates and Wm. Griffith and Abraham Jackson came into Center Twp., Monroe Co., Oh. at the same time, between 1805 & 1812. As my line comes through Abraham, I have never done any research on the Stephens line. I will see what I can find out for you the next time I go to the Ohio Genealogical Society. If you want the Jackson info that I have, I'll be glad to send it to you. Barbara
