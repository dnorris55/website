SIXTH GENERATION

60. Archibald MCDONALD was born on 14 Nov 1791 in Antrim Co. or Portadown, Armagh Co. IRE. He emigrated about 1831. He died on 19 Sep 1879 in Monroe Co, OH. He was buried in Buchanan Cemetery, Woodsfield, OH. He was a Shoe Cobbler and Farmer. He was married to Mary TATE on 6 Oct 1826 in Belmont Co, Ohio.

61. Mary TATE was born on 19 Jul 1807 in Ireland. She died on 27 Jun 1847 in Monroe Co, OH. She was buried in Buchanan Cemetery, Woodsfield, OH. Children were:

i. Eliza MCDONALD was born on 9 Jul 1827 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. She died on 20 Nov 1905 in Monroe Co, OH.
ii. Isabel MCDONALD was born on 28 Feb 1829 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. She died on 27 Jan 1869.
iii. Mary MCDONALD was born on 1 Jan 1831 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. She died on 9 Sep 1907.
iv. Jane MCDONALD was born on 15 Jan 1833 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. She died on 11 Dec 1917. She was buried in Buchanan Cemetery.
v. John Tate MCDONALD was born in 1835 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. He died on 3 Dec 1838.
vi. Margaret Tate MCDONALD was born in 1837 in Cambridge, Guernsey Co, OH. She died on 19 Nov 1838.
vii. Susan Finney MCDONALD was born on 17 Jun 1839 in Woodsfield, Monroe Co, OH. She died on 12 Apr 1916. She was buried in Yukon, OK.
viii. Agnes Louise MCDONALD was born on 20 Mar 1841 in Lewisville, Monroe Co, Oh. She died on 21 May 1917. lived in Ravenna, OH
ix. Margaret Tate MCDONALD was born on 2 Sep 1843 in Lewisville, Monroe Co, Oh. She died on 2 Jul 1927. lived in New Martinsville, WV
