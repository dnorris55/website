TENTH GENERATION

728. Hugh J. JACKSON Sr. was born on 20 May 1709 in Fermanagh,Ireland. He died on 25 Aug 1782 in Sharpsburg,Washington Co,MD. He was buried in on his farm. BIOGRAPHY: MacDonald states that Hugh Jackson saw service in the French and Indian War of 1757 - 1758 in Captain Joseph Chapline's Sharpsburg company. He is listed as a Revolutionary War Patriot. Mrs. Sarah Sinclair was awarded DAR Certificate # 145922 based on his service.

BIOGRAPHY: According to Richard Henthorn, Hugh Jackson's will is located in the Hall of Records, Annapolis, MD, Washington County Will Book, T. S. 1, page 130, dated Sep 1, 1773.
He was married to Mary UNKNOWN.
