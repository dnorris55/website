SIXTH GENERATION

54. Gilbert MCCOY was born about 1775 in NorthCumberland, PA. He died on 19 May 1857 in Cameron, Monroe Co, OH. He was buried in Cameron, Monroe, OH, Atkinson Cemetery. He was married to Mary E. ATKINSON about 1814 in Cameron, Ohio.

55. Mary E. ATKINSON was born in 1789 in North Cumberland, PA. She died on 10 Jan 1849 in Clarington, Monroe, OH. Children were:

i. Mary Ellen MCCOY was born about Apr 1815 in Monroe Co, OH. She died on 10 Mar 1874 in Monroe Co, OH.
ii. Sarah MCCOY was born about 1816. She died before Jul 1854.
iii. Rebecca MCCOY was born about 1818 in Monroe Co, OH. She died in 1875.
iv. Stephen MCCOY was born about 1820 in Monroe Co, OH. He died about 1847.
v. Nancy MCCOY was born in 1821 in Monroe Co, OH.
vi. William Albert MCCOY was born in 1823 in Monroe Co, OH. He died about 1895 in Appanoose, IA.
27 vii. Jane MCCOY.
viii. Charles MCCOY was born in 1828 in Monroe Co, OH.
ix. Louisa MCCOY was born on 9 Sep 1835 in Cameron, Monroe, OH. She died on 5 Feb 1890 in Grant, Perkins, NE.
