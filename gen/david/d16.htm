SEVENTH GENERATION

122. John TATE was born on 11 Jul 1776 in Ireland. He died on 13 Nov 1858. He was married to Elizabeth RUSK on 30 Dec 1796 in Carnmoney, County Antrim, Ireland.

123. Elizabeth RUSK was born on 28 Mar 1780 in Ireland. She died on 12 Jan 1859. Children were:

i. Agnes TATE was born on 7 Jun 1798. She died in Jun 1843.
ii. Jane (Jennie) TATE was born on 29 Oct 1800.
iii. Susanna TATE was born on 12 May 1803. She died in 1898.
iv. Eliza TATE was born on 15 Mar 1805. She died in 1892.
61 v. Mary TATE.
vi. William TATE was born on 1 Oct 1809 in Ireland. He died on 10 Jul 1857.
vii. Ann Jean TATE was born on 15 May 1812 in Ireland or Ohio. She died on 20 Jul 1889.
viii. John A. TATE was born on 1 Oct 1816 in OH. He died on 21 Jan 1857.
ix. Margaret TATE was born on 3 Oct 1819 in OH. She died on 31 May 1894.
