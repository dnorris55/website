NINTH GENERATION

366. Jacob THOMPSON was born in 1727 in Bucks/Chester Co., IRE. He died in Nov 1793 in Chester Co., PA. He was married to Ann DOWNARD in 1748 in London Grove, Chester Co., PA.

367. Ann DOWNARD died in 1800 in Salem, Fayette Co., PA. Children were:

i. Ann THOMPSON was born on 15 Apr 1749 in London Grove, Chester Co., PA. She died on 22 Apr 1819 in Fayette Co., PA.
ii. Rebecca THOMPSON was born in 1751.
iii. Margaret THOMPSON was born about 1753.
iv. Elizabeth THOMPSON was born in 1754 in Chester Co., PA. She died on 23 Jul 1832 in German Twp, Fayette Co PA. She was buried in Old Brown Cemetery near Windy Hill Cemetery, Fayette Co PA.
v. Jane THOMPSON was born in 1756 in PA. She died on 28 May 1844 in PA.
vi. James THOMPSON was born on 20 Feb 1758 in London Grove, Chester Co., PA. He died on 8 Oct 1835 in Senecaville, Guernsey Co., OH.
163 vii. Sarah B. THOMPSON.
viii. Susan "Sally" THOMPSON was born on 5 Nov 1770. She died on 10 Apr 1854 in Knox,OH.
ix. Jacob THOMPSON Jr. was born about 1760. He died in Oct 1823 in German Twp., Fayette Co., PA.
