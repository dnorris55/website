SEVENTH GENERATION

88. Abraham JACKSON Jr. was born on 19 Feb 1788 in McNallen Twp,Fayette Co, PA. He died on 10 Oct 1861 in Center Twp, Monroe County, Ohio. He was buried in Fairview Cemetery, Monroe Co, OH. He was married to Mary LEONARD.

89. Mary LEONARD was born about 1789/90 in PA. She died on 22 Sep 1852. Children were:

44 i. Silas JACKSON.
ii. Jesse JACKSON was born about 1813 in PA.
iii. Margaret JACKSON was born on 4 Aug 1814 in Fayette Co,PA. She died on 10 Sep 1895.
iv. Robert JACKSON was born on 16 Aug 1816 in Fayette Co,PA. He died.
v. Hannah JACKSON was born on 12 Dec 1818. She died about 1862.
vi. Jane JACKSON was born on 10 Feb 1821 in Monroe Co, OH.
vii. Leonard JACKSON was born on 19 Jun 1823.
viii. Sarah Ann JACKSON was born about 1825 in OH. She died on 8 Jun 1907 in Wilding, Jackson, WV.
