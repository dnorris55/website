EIGHTH GENERATION

182. Abraham JACKSON was born on 27 Jan 1759 in Hagerstown,Frederick,MD. He died on 21 Jun 1847 in Liberty Twp,Knox Co, OH. He was buried in Bird Cemetery,Liberty Twp,Knox Co, OH. BIOGRAPHY: Abraham Jackson (1759-1847) -Robert-Hugh, had 2 wives. Sarah Thompson and Sally Thompson (both daughters of Jacob Thompson and Ann Downard). They had children Aaron, Annie, Jessie, Abraham, Mary, Rebecca and Deborah. Sally had two sons George William Jackson born 15 March, 1810 at Fayette Co. PA, married Sarah Riley Cramer 20 Dec. 1844 in Knox Co. and died there 24 Sept. 1891; and Ephraim born 16 Feb. 1805.
He was married to Sarah B. THOMPSON about 1780 in PA.
