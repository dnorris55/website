SEVENTH GENERATION

120. John 2 CRAIG was born about 1780 in Bucks Co, Pa. He died on 30 Sep 1864. He was a Tavern keeper in National Pike 2 miles East of West Alexander. He resided West Finley Twp, near the WV state line. He resided 1/2 miles South of Tollgate 6 on the National Pike. He was married to Eleanor CRAIG about 1807 in Donegal, Washington, PA.

121. Eleanor CRAIG died in 1852. Children were:

i. Jane CRAIG.
ii. William CRAIG.
iii. John 3 CRAIG.
iv. Eleanor CRAIG.
v. Mary CRAIG.
vi. Samuel CRAIG.
vii. James CRAIG was born on 25 May 1825 in Donegal township, Washington Co., PA. He died. He was a farmer in 3 miles SE of West Alexander, on one of his father's farms.
60 viii. Roland CRAIG.
ix. Margaret CRAIG.
